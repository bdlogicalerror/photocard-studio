### 1. Invisible DNA Embedding
Unlike a visible logo that ruins your aesthetic, our technology mathematically *weaves* a unique ID into the actual pixels of your image. Think of it like a genetic barcode — invisible to the human eye, but instantly readable by our verification engine.

When you share a news card on Facebook, WhatsApp, or X, those platforms aggressively compress images to save bandwidth. Most watermarks get destroyed. **Ours is designed to survive.** Our ID hides in the mathematical "bones" of the image — the same frequencies that compression algorithms preserve.

If someone steals your image and reposts it:
